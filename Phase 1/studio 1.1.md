Assignment title: Visual prompt studio phase 1.1
prompt: Create a large-scale oil painting on canvas, designed as a form of social protest against homelessness and child labor. The artwork should be displayed in a widely renowned art gallery, where it becomes the center of attention for all viewers.The main message of the artwork is to raise awareness about poverty, hunger, illiteracy, and child rights.The subject matter focuses on human dignity, emotion, and social reality, including scenes of everyday struggle, loss of childhood, emotional expression, the cycle of poverty, and the presence of hope amid hardship.The artwork should be created in a representational style. Dull and earthy colors are used to represent poverty and hardship, while light and bright colors highlight the child figure to symbolize hope. A strong contrast between light and dark should be clearly visible.Movement is conveyed through expressive body postures and diagonal lines, emphasizing continuous struggle and resilience.
Vocabulary Breakdown: 
Medium: Oil paint on large-scale canvas
Meaning: The artwork aims to raise awareness about homelessness and child labor by revealing the harsh realities of poverty, hunger, illiteracy, and the denial of child rights, while also emphasizing hope and human resilience.
Subject Matter: Human dignity, emotional expression, homelessness, child labor, loss of childhood, everyday struggle, the cycle of poverty, and hope amid hardship.
Style: Representational art
